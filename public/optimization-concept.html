Optimization Testing Concept

Hypothetical test design for insurance panel content. Includes panel UI elements and conceptual multivariate test design from a UI designer perspective.

Overview

Context

Within the context of this project, a panel is a responsive container used to group related user interface elements togethor. In my current line of work, a large portion of my job is to design, and implement travel insurance product marketing onto a specified space of website real estate.

Goal

Design a conceptual multivariate test from the perspective of a somebody managing the digital portion of the build. The digital build is based on a mock DOE test design for a hypothetical airline partner website where the panel will reside.

Sections:

  • Competitive Analysis
  • Baseline Offer Mockup
  • DOE Mock Document

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed Five Websites

The competitive analysis was completed by documenting copy-text, images, and basic Yes/No functionalities for 6 insurance panels, among 3 different insurance providers, on 5 different websites.

  • Ticketmaster.comb
  • Delta.com
